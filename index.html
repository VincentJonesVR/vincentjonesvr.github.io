    Hiya! Take a look around.

    I'm Vincent Jones. I'm what many would call a level designer - though I'd prefer something like 'worldcrafter', as that is always my intent. Be it in VR or for traditional displays, original designs or ports - I always aim to create an atmosphere that will interest the player. In many ways, everything I create is for myself; I can only hope others enjoy them to a similar extent!

    Lonely Home  - 2021

    Lonely Home is a simple Source 2 environment created to test some of the expanded capabilities of the Hammer 2 editor. Primarily this scene was created to test physics props, physics doors, volumetric fog and sun beams, texture projection, and mesh combination. Still in progress.   











    Scenic Decay (7-11 Surivival) - 2016/2017

    Scenic Decay was a Left 4 Dead 2 campaign in development in 2016, but ultimately went unfinished  . The 7-11  store was the setting for a Survival gamemode map.

    South Vale - 2017/2021

    In 2017 I took on the massive project of porting the entirety of Silent Hill 2's playable area of South Vale into VRChat. Currently in the process of rebooting this project, a video of the WIP intro sequence can be found here, as well.

     





                                                 

    Lakeview Hotel - 2017/2021

    Prior to South Vale, I'd made my first Unity project porting Lakeview Hotel from Silent Hill 2 into VRChat. In 2021, I returned to redo this project with more of the skills I learned over the last few years. This world is compatible with PCVR devices and Oculus Quest devices.

      



                                                     
                                                 

    Night on The Town - 2018

    By request from a close friend, I had been aiming to recreate a few locations from the visual novel "Katawa-Shoujo". This is a pub that the main characters visit during one of the story scenes.

           


                                                        

    Rin's Room - 2019

    In the same vane as Night on The Town, Rin's Room is a recreation of the room of one of the main characters. This was a project that I attempted to reach something similar to a watercolour style with.
